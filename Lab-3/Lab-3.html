Blessings To Go

898 State Street
New Haven, CT 06511

The Review

I've had takeout from many Chinese restaurants, and keep going back to Blessings.
They consistently have the freshest and best Chinese food in New Haven.

Menu Favorites

  • Spring Roll
  • Chicken Wings
  • Vegetarian Steamed Dumplings
  • Bone-in Spare Ribs
  • Chicken and Broccoli in Garlic Sauce

Westville Pizza

883 Whalley Avenue
New Haven, CT 06511

The Review

Westville Pizza was started by a cousin of Frank Pepe, of Pepe's Pizza. It has since been sold, and the new owners and operators are Turkish. I am happy to say they have continued the same traditional Pepe's style of pizza, and it is still just as delicious. They also offer various Mediterranean dishes.

Menu Favorites

  • Cheese pizza with pepperoni
  • Calzone with anchovies and mushrooms
  • Koufta Kabob Combo
  • Harissa
  • Foxon Park Creme Soda
